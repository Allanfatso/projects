Our Product

Hodzeko, Amasi, Maas – this beloved African sour milk goes by many names, but one thing remains the same: our perfected recipe will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more of this Southern African delicacy.

Our Story

Our rich and creamy Amasi(cultured milk) is made from the finest natural ingredients to bring you the best flavour and quality. It's recipe hails all the way from Zimbabwe with a rich taste that is surprisingly familiar yet wholly unique. We use the local British milk with bacterial cultures to produce the quality Amasi that will keep you coming back. Don’t hesitate to try it...

Our Commitment

Because we care about your health and wellbeing, we have taken the greatest care to ensure that our Amasi promotes your gut health and promotes a healthy immune system. Our home-made yoghurt is only made with natural ingredients and British farm milk.

WHY CHIDO DAIRIES?

Our rich Amasi is best enjoyed as a chilled desert, an addition to your breakfast cereal or as a side for dinner. But why stop there? Let your culinary creativity soar – Amasi is the perfect ingredient to elevate countless recipes and bring a touch of Southern African tradition to every dish.
